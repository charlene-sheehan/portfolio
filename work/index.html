Designing Started Young

I first started designing as a child. My father worked in the golf manufacturing industry, which often meant he was away for the holidays in China. I designed my own e-cards for him every holiday. Those cards lead to a golf logo design since he wasn't any good at the computer. My 16th birthday was very sweet when he gave me my first driver, which was imprinted with my logo. A lot has changed since I was a teen, but my enthusiasm for creating and capabilities to do so have only grown.
Rather than flexing my imagination on PaintShopPro 8, I'm working with up-to-date professional-grade programs to make quality web work inside and out. Good design can't function without well thought out coding.
